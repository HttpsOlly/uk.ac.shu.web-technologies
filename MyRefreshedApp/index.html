Geography

Finland is a Nordic country situated in North-Eastern Europe, possessing land borders with Sweden, Norway and Russia. It has a population of 5.6 million people, of which 650,000 people live in the capital Helsinki.

Comprising predominantly of forests at nearly 75% of the land being covered with trees, Finland is one of the world's most forest covered countries, and Europe's most forest covered.
